The Ancient Art

Why Shou Sugi Ban?

A 400-year-old Japanese technique, perfected by fire. The most beautiful, durable, and sustainable cladding ever created.

Fire Resistant
The charring process creates a natural fire barrier. BAL-12.5 standard as default, BAL-29 available on request. Ideal for bushfire-prone regions.
Zero Maintenance
Unlike paint or render, charred timber never needs repainting. The carbonised surface is self-sealing and improves with age. 75–100yr service life.
Carbon Locked
The charring process locks carbon into the timber permanently, making Shou Sugi Ban one of the most carbon-negative cladding materials available.
Stunning Aesthetics
Three char depths — Light, Medium, Heavy — all included in base price. The dramatic black facade commands every landscape and never looks dated.
